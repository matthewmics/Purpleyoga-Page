  • Purple Yoga Hawaii
    Eaton Square
    438 Hobron Lane, Suite 312
    Honolulu, HI 96815
    (808) 944-8585 info@purpleyoga.com
  • Pedestrian main entrance (above) leads to a courtyard, look up right past the banyon tree, take stairs to our studio on the third floor (Vista Level). For the elevator, go left past the staircase.

    Pedestrian back entrance on Makaoe Lane (no street sign), just past 1717 Ala Wai Boulevard.
  • Car Parking
  • Free street parking on Ala Wai Boulevard, Kaioo Drive, and Hobron Lane between Lipeepee St and Ala Moana Boulevard.

    Free public parking at Ala Moana Shopping Center, corner of Ala Moana Boulevard and Atkinson Drive. Walk is 10 minutes.

    Metered street parking (50 cents for 10 minutes) along Hobron Lane between Lipeepee Street and Ena Road.
  • Public parking building (above) at the intersection of Hobron Lane and Lipeepee St, 300 feet west of pedestrian entrance. Open 24 hours, seven days. Grace period 20 minutes.

    Park on levels 2, 3 or 4 and walk to the far east end of each level where walkways lead to the courtyard. Take the stairs or elevator to our studio on the third floor (Vista Level).

    Validation sticker available from us at cost: $4.00 two hours. Cash only.

    Eaton Square Shopping Center has a large grocery/deli.
  • Bikes and Bus
  • Bikes and motorbikes not permitted inside the Square. Bikes can be locked to a wall bar at the back entrance (see left).

    Bus from Waikiki hotelstake #2 from Kuhio Avenue and get off just after McCully Street, then a four-minute walk.

  • Studio Information
  • The studio is approx. 1300 sq ft and the main floor is approx. 700 sq ft. There is a large entrance and reception area, two changing rooms and another empty room approx. 100sq ft.
